About

From engineering
to design

I came from civil engineering (AutoCAD, SketchUp, structural thinking) and at some point realised I was more interested in how people use things than how they're built. That shift led me into UI/UX, and it's where I've been focused ever since.

I work across app and website design: wireframes, user flows, prototypes, motion design. I'm comfortable going from early research and usability testing all the way through to a polished, production-ready interface. During my MSc I also got into AR and VR, which genuinely changed how I think about spatial design.

On the technical side, I code too. HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap. So I understand what's actually buildable and can work closely with developers without things getting lost in translation.
